About

I'm Yanjaa Ganzorig. I write to think — these essays are working notes, not finished positions. My views change, and I try to let them.

I'm interested in how people make sense of the world: through attention, through language, through the slow accumulation of experience. I'm drawn to ideas that don't resolve cleanly, and to the discipline of sitting with uncertainty long enough to learn something from it.

This site is a place to put ideas that feel worth sharing. I write about technology, thinking, and whatever else I can't stop turning over in my mind.

You can reach me at hello@yanjaa.com.
